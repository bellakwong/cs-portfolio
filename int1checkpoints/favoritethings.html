Favorite Things

Musicals

I love multiple musicals and have an obsession that’s so bad that it’s probably unhealthy. I love the characters, the story, and the meanings behind the lyrics. Pop music doesn’t have that compelling story that makes you cry, wonder what will happen next, or move you so much that it changes your view of the world. Some favorite musicals include Dear Evan Hansen, Hamilton, Be More Chill, The Book of Mormon, and In the Heights.

Books

I love books for the stories, the plot, the betrayal, and the characters. The character development and how it aligns to the plot is what makes the book worth reading. My favorite books are the ones that make you cry, laugh, and smile. Novels can teach, connect, and relate so much to our lives. Just like musicals, they can move you and change your perspective on the world. A few of my favorite books include An Ember in the Ashes, A Torch Against the Night, The Outsiders, Cress, Flowers for Algernon, and House of Secrets.
